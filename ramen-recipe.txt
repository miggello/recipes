Ingredients:
1 packet of ramen noodles with seasoning included
1-3 tablespoons peanut butter
1-3 teaspoons srirachi sauce.

Directions:
1) Add noodle block to large bowl.
2) Remove seasoning packet from bowl, set aside for later.
3) Add water to noodles, making sure to cover them and have extra
4) Cook 5-10 minutes in microwave.
5) Add seasoning from packet.
6) Add peanut butter to taste
7) Add srirachi sauce to taste.
8) Enjoy!
